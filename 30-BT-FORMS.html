simple collapsible

CLICK ON THE BUTTON TO TOGGLE BETWEEN SHOWING AND HIDING CONTENT.

this is a [pargraph]

the collapse class indicates a collapsible element(a div in our example),this is the content that will be shown or hidden with a click of a button

to control(show/hide)the collapsible content,add the data-toggle="collapse" attribute to an anchir tag or button element.then add the data target="#id"attribute to connect the button with the collapsible content

Note:for anchor element,you can use the href attribute instead of the data target attribute
